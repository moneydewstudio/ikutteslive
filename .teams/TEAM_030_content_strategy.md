### 1. **Intent Split**
- **70%** Practice/Test Prep (SKD keywords) — **MATCHES YOUR APP**
- **30%** Job Discovery (Formasi keywords) — **UPPER FUNNEL**

### 2. **Volume Concentration**
- Top 10 keywords = ~60% of total search volume
- "Try out" and "Soal" dominate ( Indonesians search for practice tests)

### 3. **Kedinasan Opportunity**
- "kedinasan" modifiers appear frequently
- Civil service schools = different exam format
- Separate content cluster opportunity

### 4. **Your App Alignment**
✅ **Perfect Match:** All SKD/practice keywords
